Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?
The weekly plan includes a 3-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged. After the trial ends, your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
What's included in the lifetime plan?
The lifetime plan includes all premium features with no recurring payments. You'll receive lifetime access to all current and future premium features, including updates and new content.
Is family sharing available?
Currently, family sharing is not available for our subscription plans. Each family member would need their own subscription to access premium features.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What if I have technical issues?
For technical support or subscription issues, please visit our Support Center or contact us through the app.
