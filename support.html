Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset the app if it's not working properly?

Try these steps in order: 1) Close the app completely and reopen it, 2) Restart your device, 3) Delete and reinstall the app (your data will be preserved if you're signed in), 4) Contact support if the issue persists.

How do I enable daily Bible verse notifications?

Go to the Home screen ‚Üí Tap "Notification Settings" ‚Üí Tap "Request Permission" ‚Üí Allow notifications in your device settings. You can also test notifications using the "Test Notifications" button.

How do I manage my subscription?

To manage your subscription: 1) Open your device's Settings, 2) Tap your Apple ID, 3) Tap "Subscriptions", 4) Find "Bible Life Verses" and manage your subscription there. You can cancel, change plans, or update payment methods.

How do I search for specific Bible verses?

Use the Bible tab and try these methods: 1) Smart Search - type topics like "anxiety" or "hope", 2) Reference Search - enter specific references like "John 3:16", 3) Browse - navigate by book, chapter, and verse.

How do I use the Spiritual Guidance feature?

Tap the "Spiritual Guidance" tab and start typing your question or concern. You can also use the suggested prompts like "I'm feeling overwhelmed" or "I need guidance in my relationship" to get started.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Most features work offline, including Bible verses, topics, and spiritual guidance. You'll need an internet connection for initial app download and updates.

How do I find verses for specific life situations?

Use the "Topics" tab to browse categories like "Anxiety & Stress", "Relationships", "Work & Career", etc. Each topic contains relevant Bible verses and guidance for that situation.

Is my prayer data private?

Yes, your prayers and personal reflections are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. Your privacy is important to us.
